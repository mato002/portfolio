# How to Update Your EmailJS Template

## Step 1: Copy the Professional Template

1. Open the file: `EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_PROFESSIONAL.html`
2. Select all content (Ctrl + A)
3. Copy it (Ctrl + C)

## Step 2: Update in EmailJS Dashboard

1. Go to: https://dashboard.emailjs.com/
2. Navigate to: **Email Templates**
3. Open your template: `template_gn3n04j`
4. Click on the **"Content"** tab
5. Click **"Edit Content"** button
6. Select all existing content and delete it
7. Paste the new professional template (Ctrl + V)

## Step 3: Verify Template Variables

Make sure your template uses these variables (they're already included):
- `{{from_name}}` - Sender's name
- `{{from_email}}` - Sender's email
- `{{message}}` - The message content
- `{{date}}` - Submission date/time

## Step 4: Save Settings

1. In the **Settings** tab, verify:
   - **Subject:** "New Contact Form Submission" (or your preferred subject)
   - **To Email:** `matechodhis@gmail.com`
   - **From Email:** Use default or set your email
   - **Reply To:** `{{from_email}}` or `matechodhis@gmail.com`

2. Click the blue **"Save"** button at the top

## Step 5: Test

1. Click **"Test It"** button to send a test email
2. Or submit the contact form on your portfolio
3. Check your email to see the new professional design
